    Offline maps

    Save the map tiles for the whole route to your phone while you're on WiFi. After that, the entire app — maps, route and all stop content — works with no connection and no roaming charges.

    ✓ Offline maps saved. You're good to go.

    Install on your iPhone

    1. Open this page in Safari (not Chrome).
    2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow).
    3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”.
    4. Open it from the home screen icon — it runs full-screen like an app.
    5. Tap “Download offline maps” above while on WiFi.
